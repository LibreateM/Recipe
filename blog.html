Mediterranean diet

The Mediterranean diet emphasizes plant-based foods such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, and nuts. This dish features a variety of colorful vegetables and fruits, including what appears to be figs and pomegranate seeds. Olive oil is the primary source of fat in the Mediterranean diet, and lean fish and seafood are recommended in moderate amounts. Red and processed meats are consumed in limited quantities, and wine, typically red, may be consumed in small portions with meals. This dietary pattern is associated with various health benefits, including a reduced risk of cardiovascular disease and other chronic conditions.

Umami Burger is a hamburger chain founded in Los Angeles, California, in 2009 by Adam Fleischman.The name "Umami" refers to the savory taste, which is one of the five basic tastes and is often described as a pleasant, brothy, or meaty flavor.Umami Burgers often feature ingredients that are high in umami, such as shiitake mushrooms, caramelized onions, roasted tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, and a special "umami ketchup".The chain aimed to offer a "fine-dining fast food" experience, providing a sophisticated take on the classic hamburger.
