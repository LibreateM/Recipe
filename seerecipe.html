😋Yummy Rawa Dosa😋

Alice Ara

Steps For Preparing

Step 1:  In a large bowl, combine semolina, rice flour, and all-purpose flour. Mix well and add salt, cumin seeds, chopped green chilies, grated ginger, and a pinch of asafoetida.

Step 2:  Add yogurt and gradually pour water while stirring continuously to make a smooth, thin batter. Let it rest for 15–20 minutes.

Step 3: Heat a non-stick or cast-iron tawa (griddle) over medium heat. Lightly grease with oil.

Step 4: Stir the batter well. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot tawa, starting from the edges and allowing it to spread naturally in a thin layer.

Step 5: Sprinkle chopped onions, coriander leaves, and optional garnishes over the dosa. Drizzle a little oil around the edges.

Step 6: Cook until the bottom turns golden and crisp. Fold the dosa and serve hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or tomato chutney.

  Ingredients 

01

Base Ingredients

1 cup semolina (Rawa/Sooji) 1/4 cup rice flour 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (optional for crispiness)

02

Liquids

2–2.5 cups water (adjust for batter consistency) 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)

03

Flavorings & Spices

1–2 green chilies (finely chopped) 1 tsp ginger (grated) 1/2 tsp cumin seeds A pinch of asafoetida (hing) Salt to taste 1–2 green chilies (finely chopped) 1 tsp ginger (grated) 1/2 tsp cumin seeds A pinch of asafoetida (hing) Salt to taste

04

Garnishes (Optional)

Fresh coriander leaves (chopped) Finely chopped onions Grated carrot or chopped capsicum (for extra flavor and texture)
